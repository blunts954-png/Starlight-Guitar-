Identity

We don’t do plastic.

Iron and wood. Soul over SKU.

At Skylight Guitars, we aren’t interested in the mass-produced inventory you’ll find at corporate big-box stores. We deal in iron and wood—quality American-made, used, and vintage gear chosen for tone and integrity, not for turnover.

Based in the heart of Bakersfield, we curate the gear that helped build the Sound. Whether it’s an estate collection of pre-war acoustics or tour-grade equipment that’s seen more miles than a Greyhound bus, we only move instruments with a story worth telling.

We’re gearheads. We’re history nerds. We’re here so the next generation plays something worth keeping.
