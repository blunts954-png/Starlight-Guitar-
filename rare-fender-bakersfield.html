Fender & the Bakersfield Sound

Target intent: Pre-CBS Fender guitars Central CA, tweed-era drip, and workhorse Teles that earned their buckle rash on real stages.

Twang isn’t a setting—it’s lineage. We bring in Fender guitars and amps that pair with steel and story: blackguards, rosewood-board workhorses, and the occasional holy-grail amp that still smells like tubes and time.
