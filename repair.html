Electric guitars & basses

From vintage Gibson/Fender neck angles to modern floating trems, we treat electrics as systems: neck, frets, nut, electronics, and hardware all have to agree under string tension.

  • Level/dress/crown frets; refret when life is gone
  • Truss rod adjustments with seasonal movement in mind
  • Nut replacement (composite, bone) for stable tuning
  • Pickup height, grounding, and tour-grade wiring
  • Bridge/trem setup—block, deck, or float to your playing style
  • Setup for gigging action: buzz-free, bend-friendly, consistent intonation

Acoustic guitars

Acoustics are air pumps. We respect bridge plate integrity, brace patterns, and the long-term health of the top—especially on older boxes where the wrong “quick fix” can kill value.

  • Neck resets and saddle work for proper geometry
  • Crack stabilization and humidity coaching
  • Bridge reglue, pin fit, and intonation optimization
  • Fretwork for light strings or fingerstyle setups
  • Pickup installs for stage—without killing acoustic voice
