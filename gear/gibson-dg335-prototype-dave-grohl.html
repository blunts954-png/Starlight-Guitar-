The story

Dave Grohl's first prototype of the DG-355 from Gibson's Custom Shop—inspired by his favorite guitar, the Trini Lopez '67 signature. Dual diamond f-holes, laminated maple top and back with poplar rims, solid maple center block, mahogany neck with a rounded '50s ES-355 carve, rosewood board, Burstbuckers, Firebird headstock, and a truss rod cover engraved with Dave's custom 606.

“This Prototype was played on Echoes, Silence, Patience & Grace as well as toured from 2005 until the first production guitars were delivered in 2007 and then the prototype was retired.”

— From the original owner’s statement (abridged)
